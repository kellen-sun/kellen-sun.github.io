⛰ What It's About

Fifth Business: A fifth character that is neither the main character or his love interest, nor the villain and his love interest. However, this fifth character is crucial to the development of the plot and even holds the key that will solve the conflict.

Book spans a long time: starts in 1908 December 27th, (narrator in 10 years old and ends in 1970)

Telling the story from the perspective of an older man looking back on the man he was.

He was offended by something during his retirement. He got offended because of a news article? was published by the prestigious private school he worked at. He’s writing the report/book to the school’s headmaster. He’s trying to prove to the headmaster that he is more than what the story makes him to be, that he lived a life worth living, that he loved, hated, and experienced many aspects of life that the article tries to deny. Dunny tries to prove that he’s fifth business.

The fifth business character has the key to the mystery

Town is Deptford

Narrator is Dunny (Dunstable), his frenemy is Percy Boyd Staunton.

When he’s 10 years old (+7months), Percy aimed a snowball at him, he dodged, it hit Mrs. Dempster who the night of gave birth to Paul Dempster. Later Mrs. Dempster goes crazy. Percy refuses to take responsibility for the affair and this leads Dunny to take most of the guilt of causing the early birth of Paul.

Mr. Dempster (Amasa Dempster) isn’t very masculine and Mrs. Dempster lacks the femininity known of those who can do chores around the house. → Maybe unpolarized relationship

The Dempsters got a lot of help from Dunny and his mother.

Dunny spending time helping Mrs. Dempster (decently young) makes him develop some love for her. He also likes a classmate.

Dunny starts working at the library and reads of a book teaching magic from which he tries learning tricks (including making an egg appear out of no where). His mom beats him for stealing her egg and denying it.

Dunny continues pursuing magic. Learns card tricks and coin tricks. Shows them to Paul who learns the sleight of hand really quickly. Paul remains grateful for his ability and that Dunny can teach him as Paul still cannot read. (Paul 4yo now) Dunny at some times considered killing Paul to “rid the world of a precocious nuisance” (p39) However, Dunny was in a part of his life where he strongly valued gratitude and admiration towards him as he lacked it from other people. he got that admiration from paul. (Maybe cause of the beating from his mother)

Paul’s father learns that he’s trained in some kind of ‘witchcraft’ from Dunny. And orders Dunny to stay away from his family.

One day, Mrs. Dempster is gone and Mr. Dempster and other men of the village are sent to try and find her. This group includes Dunny. They search a small granite mountain near their village that’s abandoned because of the railroad system. Dunny finds Mrs. Dempster in the midst of having sex with a beggar. When asked why she did it she says: “He was very civil, ‘Masa. And he wanted it so badly.” p48

Then Willie, Dunny’s brother gets terribly sick basically dies, and Dunny fetches for Mrs. Dempster who somehow brings him back to life.

Dunny leaves for the war (before which he dated Leola Cruikshank). He almost dies and loses consciousness for 5 months after he rushed for a machine gun and took it out at the third battle of Yvres in November of 1917.

p87: When receiving medals of victoria cross from the king. “We are public icons, we two: he an icon of kingship, and I an icon of heroism, unreal yet very necessary; we have obligations above what is merely personal, and to let personal feelings obscure the obligations would be failing in one’s duty” …. “I have tried to think charitably of people in prominent positions of one kind or another; we cast them in roles, and it is only right to consider them as players, without trying to discredit them with knowledge of their off-stage life—unless they drag it into the middle of the stage themselves.” (He’s about 20)

His relationship with Diana has the dynamic that Diana nursed him back to life and taught him so many things helping him on his way back to normalcy after he got injured in the war. Therefore, she’s more of a mother to him than wife.

Diana is a couple years older than Dunny

Dunny returns to Deptford and is warmly welcomed (Percy and Leola are engaged now)
